Sydneys Riders

Sydneys Riders is a communtiy of motorbike riders and for the last year and a half I have worked along side the founders, helping them with there digital platform and graphics. Designer for Sydneys Riders to me isn’t just a side project, I am apart a community that I am actively promoting and partaking in. My position allows me to be a creative thrill seeker.   • Creating the logo and design campaign for their annual film festival
  • Marketing material to send to sponsors
