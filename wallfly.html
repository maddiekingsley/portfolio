WallFly

A web application that helps improve communication between agents, owners and tenants

Design Computing Studio (DECO3801)

The aim of DECO3801 was to build and redefine a product created from a pervious course (DECO3800). Myself and a team of five were given the proposal WallFly. WallFly is an online property management platform through which agents, tenants and owners can fulfill many of their rental obligations. WallFly aims to combine several key services into a single, transparent platform for all three parties to use. After substantial user research and investigation we found that agents, owners and tenants all felt as though there were issues with their communication with the other parties in their rental relationships.

My Role and Responsibilites

My role as a UX/UI designer I was in charge of perfomring user testing, creating wireframes, user flow, high fidelity prototypes, design documnets, interface design and interviews with users and the client.
