Rainbow Fruit Logo

In the small town of Rainbow Beach in Queensland, a fruit and vegetable barn has a logo designed by myself. My first design project which is now present throughout the entire store.

Rainbow Fruit is an effective logo for it is distinctive, appropriate, practical, graphic, simple in form and conveys an intended message. Designing a logo for a fruit barn, it was appropriate to use a colour scheme relevent to fruit and the shape of the text to display an apples features something unique without being overdrawn.
